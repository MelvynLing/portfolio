My name is Melvyn Ling and I am a junior front end Developer based out of Southern Ontario. I am an advid learner with an ever curious mindset, I've always been one to look for and exploring new horizons, which is exactly how I got involved in Web Development. I got all caught up in web development about 2 years ago - I had taken a course online and absolutely fell in love with it. Since then, I've been doing all that I can to strengthen my web development skillset, as well as garner new skills. In November of 2018, I graduated from the University of Toronto's BTS Coding Bootcamp, a 6 months intensive web development program aimed at making one career ready for the web development industry.

In addition of this, I also have a BA in Political Science from the McMaster University. While not an ordinary background for a web developer, I believe that it provides me with a number of strengths. One of which would be interpersonal and communication skills, which allow me to connect to others and be perceptive to their needs, wants, and views. It has also provided me with a strong critical thinking background, allowing me to question which approaches are best suited and why they are suited for a particular web development need. While not typical for a web developer, my educational background as certainly made me who I am today allowing me to be creative and think outside of the box of solutions.
